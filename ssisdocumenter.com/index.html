Do you spend your time in ETL and System Integration?...

We too and we found that very often it is very hard to manage large SSIS libraries inherited from various developers over time. Some times you just need to get a general overvie of the package to get quick understanding usages and manin components. And we decided to make our work more productive and organized.

This is how SSIS Documenter was born. It will let you document SSIS Pacakges one by one or as a part of your build process.

Easy Documentation

SSIS Documenter is integrated to the Business Intelligence Studio, the main environment for designing and debuggin of SSIS pacakges. You can generate documentation with a right click of a mouse without loading additional programs..
